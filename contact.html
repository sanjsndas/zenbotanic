How to Schedule a Gardening Consultation or Service

Planning your garden refresh or maintenance is easy with ZenBotanic:

  • Contact us via phone or email specifying your service interest.
  • Our team will guide you through available dates and explain service options and pricing.
  • We will schedule an initial consultation if desired, to understand your space and preferences.
  • Upon agreement, service scheduling will be confirmed and carried out by our expert gardening team.

We recommend early scheduling during peak seasons (spring and fall) to secure preferred dates.
