Privacy Policy

Welcome to ZenBotanic Gardening's Privacy Policy. Your privacy and trust are of utmost importance to us. This comprehensive policy explains in detail how we collect, use, protect, and disclose your personal information through your interaction with our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services effectively. The categories include:

  • Personal Identification Information: such as your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and other contact details when you subscribe, register, or communicate with us.
  • Technical Data: including IP address, browser type and version, time zone setting, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and platform, and other technology on the devices you use to access our website.
  • Usage Data: information about how you use our website, including the pages you visit, time spent on pages, clicks, navigation paths, and other engagement metrics.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: small data files stored on your device used to analyze web traffic, customize user experience, and track interactions.
  • Communications: copies of your correspondence, feedback, queries, or service requests sent to us via email or online forms.
2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, personalize, and maintain our gardening services, respond to inquiries, and assist with service requests.
  • Communication: To contact you with newsletters, marketing, and promotional materials if you have opted in.
  • Improvement: To analyze website usage and improve the quality, functionality, and user experience of our site and services.
  • Security: To ensure the security of our website, prevent fraud, and protect our rights and property.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, court orders, or government regulations and respond to law enforcement requests.
3. Consent

By using our website or services, you consent to our collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. Where required by law, we seek your explicit consent before collecting or processing your personal data.

4. Cookies and Similar Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies, such as web beacons and pixels, to improve user experience and analyze traffic patterns. Cookies may be classified as:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality such as navigation and access to secure sections.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous information on how visitors use the site, enabling us to improve load times and content.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences such as language, location, or login details.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and track campaign effectiveness, typically placed by third-party advertisers.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences, including blocking or deleting cookies, but this may affect website usability.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

ZenBotanic Gardening does not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data in limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors assisting with website hosting, data analysis, marketing, customer service, and payment processing who are obligated to keep your data confidential.
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, subpoena, or governmental request, or to enforce our Terms of Service or protect our rights.
  • Business Transfers: In event of mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales, your information might be transferred as part of the transaction with continued privacy protections.
6. Data Security

We take comprehensive technical, physical, and administrative measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • SSL encryption on our website to safeguard data transmission.
  • Secure servers with firewall protection.
  • Regular security audits and employee training.
  • Access controls ensuring only authorized personnel can view your personal information.
7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, including legal compliance, dispute resolution, and enforcement of agreements.

8. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

You have several rights under applicable laws regarding your personal data including:

  • Access: You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request to correct inaccuracies or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: In certain circumstances, you may request deletion of your data.
  • Restriction: You have the right to restrict processing of your data under some conditions.
  • Data Portability: You may request a copy of your data in a portable, commonly used format.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: If processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time without affecting past processing.
  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of marketing communications at any time by following unsubscribe instructions.
  • Lodging Complaints: You have the right to file complaints with relevant privacy authorities if you believe your rights are violated.

To exercise any of these rights or for inquiries, please contact us at contact@zenbotanic.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children without parental consent. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately so we can delete such data.

10. International Transfers

Your information may be stored or processed in countries outside of your own. We take all necessary measures to ensure that data transferred internationally receives an adequate level of protection consistent with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

11. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites operated by third parties. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

12. Updates to Our Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy to address changes in our practices, legal requirements, or technology. We will notify visitors to our website by posting the revised policy and updating the effective date below. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
